AGENT_AVIATION_LEASE — Aircraft Leasing Integrity Module

Market: $250B+ assets under management, 50%+ of global fleet leased UK Connection: Dublin + London are global leasing hubs (AerCap, SMBC, GECAS, Avolon are top lessors) Key Players: AerCap, SMBC Aviation Capital, Avolon, BOC Aviation, CDB Aviation

1. Mission

Structural audit of aircraft lease lifecycle - from contract to redelivery. Detect maintenance reserve manipulation, redelivery condition fraud, and lessee performance anomalies.

2. Detection Architecture

2.1 Lease Return (Redelivery) Forensics

  • R1 — Return Condition Gap: Contractual return condition vs. actual aircraft status
  • R2 — Maintenance Reserve Balance: Reserve payments vs. actual maintenance performed
  • R3 — Records Transfer: Technical records completeness at redelivery

2.2 Lessee Performance Audit

  • L1 — Covenant Compliance: Financial covenant structural testing
  • L2 — Maintenance Program Adherence: Lessee's actual maintenance vs. approved maintenance program
  • L3 — Utilization Monitoring: Cycles/hours flown vs. lease agreement limits

2.3 Asset Valuation Integrity

  • V1 — Appraisal Manipulation: Independent appraiser vs. submitted valuation patterns
  • V2 — Half-Life Manipulation: Engine/APU half-life claims vs. actual shop visit records
  • V3 — Damage History: Undisclosed damage events on asset during lease term

3. Proprietary Indicators

Indicator Description Threshold
RETURN_G actual_condition - contractual_return >$1M gap = RISK
RESERVE_B reserve_paid - maint_performed >15% = MISMATCH
COVENANT_R actual_financial - covenant_limit <5% margin = WARNING
