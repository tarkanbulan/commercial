AGENT_LOG_MULTI — Multimodal Logistics & Freight Audit Module

Market: Global freight forwarding $200B+. Freight audit market $5B+. Key Players: Kuehne+Nagel, DHL Global Forwarding, DB Schenker, DSV, Expeditors

1. Mission

End-to-end multimodal shipment integrity: freight forwarder audit, transit time verification, modal shift claims, carbon reporting, and freight payment auditing.

2. Detection Architecture

2.1 Freight Forwarder Performance Audit

  • F1 — Transit Time: Quoted vs. actual door-to-door transit. Systematic over-quoting
  • F2 — Mode Selection: Shipper paid for air but goods moved sea (mode arbitrage)
  • F3 — Carrier Selection: Forwarder charges premium carrier rate but uses budget carrier

2.2 Transit Time Forensics

  • T1 — Segment Breakdown: Each leg (pickup, main, delivery) vs. industry benchmark
  • T2 — Dwell Time: Time at facility vs. operational requirement
  • T3 — Delay Root Cause: Valid (force majeure) vs. avoidable (forwarder inefficiency)

2.3 Modal Shift Verification

  • M1 — Modal Claim: Shipper invoiced for air or sea but actual mode determined from tracking
  • M2 — Carbon Impact: Modal shift environmental claims vs. actual emissions
  • M3 — Cost-Revenue: Carbon cost vs. modal price differential

2.4 Carbon Reporting (Scope 3)

  • C1 — GLEC Compliance: Global Logistics Emissions Council framework adherence
  • C2 — Allocation Method: Tonne-km vs. actual fuel-based calculation manipulation
  • C3 — Factor Manipulation: Emission factor selection (default vs. actual operator data)

2.5 Freight Audit and Payment (FAP)

  • P1 — Duplicate Payment: Same invoice paid twice across different entities
  • P2 — Rate Validation: Invoiced rate vs. contract rate. >2% variance = leakage
  • P3 — Accessorial: Extra charges (liftgate, residential, inside delivery) validity

3. Proprietary Indicators

Indicator Description Threshold
MODE_G invoiced_mode - actual_mode mismatch = ARBITRAGE
TRANSIT_Q quoted_days - actual_days >20% = SYSTEMATIC
CARBON_D reported_emissions - actual_calculation >10% = GREENWASH
FAP_L paid_rate - contract_rate >2% = LEAKAGE
