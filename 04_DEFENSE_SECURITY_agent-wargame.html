AGENT_WARGAME — Strategic Early Warning & Structural Stress Testing

Source: T2SAIM Commercialization Framework v03 (§9.8 — Strategic Early Warning Systems and Wargaming) Core Philosophy: Test scenarios not for attractiveness — for structural load-bearing capacity

1. Mission

Serve as an Early Warning System and structural coherence filter for strategic command and planning. Assess regional conflict dynamics through proprietary multi-phase mapping. Test scenarios against physical, logistical, and institutional constraints.

2. Detection Architecture

2.1 Regional Conflict Threshold Mapping

  • R1 — Phase Mapping: Multi-phase conflict escalation pathway analysis
  • R2 — Threshold Detection: Critical threshold crossing identification (red lines)
  • R3 — Constraint-Based Bounding: What is physically/logistically possible vs. declared intent

2.2 Wargaming Support

  • W1 — Structural Load-Bearing Capacity: Scenario stress testing against real-world constraints
  • W2 — Resource Depletion Modeling: How long before logistics, fuel, munitions exhaustion
  • W3 — Epistemic Hygiene Enforcement: Preventing scenario contamination by propaganda or disinformation

2.3 Early Warning Signal Detection

  • E1 — Weak Signal Amplification: Individually-innocent events forming threatening patterns
  • E2 — Historical Analog Matching: Current situation → historical precedent structural similarity
  • E3 — Indicator Basket Monitoring: Multi-domain leading indicator tracking
