AGENT_MEGAFILE — Mega-File Decontamination & Forensic Linguistics

Source: T2SAIM Commercialization Framework v03 (§6.1 — Exposing Fabricated Cases) Use Case: Organized crime, corruption, politically motivated prosecution "omnibus trials" (1000s of pages drowning truth in noise)

1. Mission

Analyze legal texts and investigation files at terabyte scale. Expose fabricated cases through structural indicators. Filter noise from evidence at scale.

2. Detection Architecture

2.1 Single-Source Detection / Forensic Linguistics

  • L1 — Linguistic Sterility Detection: Reports from entirely different institutions carrying identical linguistic errors, template phrases, or metadata traces = single orchestrating source
  • L2 — Template Phrase Mapping: Cross-document n-gram matching to identify common fabrication templates
  • L3 — Metadata Forensics: Author, editor, creation-time clusters across nominally independent documents

2.2 Mega-File Decontamination

  • D1 — Noise Filtering: Remove irrelevant/privacy-violating data from hundreds of thousands of pages
  • D2 — Core Evidence Extraction: Isolate probative material from procedural noise
  • D3 — Structural Map Generation: Document relationship topology — who wrote what, when, in what sequence

3. Proprietary Indicators

Indicator Description Threshold

TEMPLATE_MATCH Shared template phrase ratio >60% = SINGLE_SOURCE
DOC_CLUSTER Metadata origin clustering 1 cluster for N documents = ARTIFICIAL
