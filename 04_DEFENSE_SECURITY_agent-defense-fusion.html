Source: T2SAIM Commercialization Framework v03 (§9.1-9.6 — Defense Architecture) Context: DoD "AI-first" warfighting doctrines, zero error margin

1. Mission

Test structural coherence across defense data blocks: naval operations, air force sorties, drone ecosystems, border security, logistics, personnel security. Does NOT replace command decisions — surfaces high-risk anomaly zones.

2. Detection Modules

2.1 Naval Forces: Sensor-Shipyard-Platform Fusion

  • N1 — Maintenance Cycle Coherence: Platform maintenance history vs. operational tempo
  • N2 — Shipyard Schedule Integrity: Modernization schedule vs. actual platform availability
  • N3 — Ordnance Flow Testing: Critical stock behavior vs. declared mission readiness

2.2 Air Forces: Sortie-Ordnance-Alarm Chain

  • A1 — Sortie Planning Integrity: Planned vs. actual sortie rates
  • A2 — Ordnance-Platform-Profile Alignment: Mission profile vs. ordnance loading match
  • A3 — Alarm Chain Coherence: Early warning → engagement → radar → post-mission report consistency

2.3 Drone (UAV/UCAV) Anti-Spoofing

  • D1 — Flight Record Integrity: GPS, sensor, targeting, post-mission report chain flow
  • D2 — Spoofed Signal Detection: Inconsistent sensor readings indicating signal manipulation

2.4 Border Security Multi-Sensor Fusion

  • B1 — Weak Signal Integration: Smuggling crossings, tunnel activity, irregular clustering surfaced from individually-normal events
  • B2 — Cross-Sensor Coherence: Radar, thermal, seismic, comms intercept alignment testing
