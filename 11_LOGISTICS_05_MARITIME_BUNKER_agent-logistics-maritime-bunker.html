AGENT_LOG_BUNKER — Marine Fuel (Bunker) Integrity Module

Market: Global bunker fuel market $120B+. IMO 2020 sulphur cap drives compliance costs. Key Hubs: Singapore, Rotterdam, Fujairah, Houston. UK: Milford Haven, Immingham

1. Mission

Detect bunker fraud: short delivery, fuel switching, sulphur content manipulation, and scrubber compliance misrepresentation.

2. Detection Architecture

2.1 Short Delivery / Quantity Fraud

  • Q1 — MFM Manipulation: Mass Flow Meter reading vs. soundings/sludge comparison
  • Q2 — Bunker Delivery Note: BDN figures vs. actual ROB (Remaining On Board) change
  • Q3 — Temperature Correction: Volume correction for temperature (fuel expands with heat)

2.2 Fuel Quality / Switching

  • F1 — Sulphur Content: Declared %S vs. independent analysis >0.1% IMO limit
  • F2 — Fuel Switching: Scrubber claiming compliance but burning high-sulphur fuel
  • F3 — Waste Fuel: Used lube oil mixed into fuel oil (torque/power impact)

2.3 Scrubber Compliance

  • S1 — Open/Closed Loop: Open loop discharge in restricted waters (ECA compliance)
  • S2 — Washwater Monitoring: pH, PAH, turbidity data manipulation
  • S3 — EGCS Log: Scrubber operation record vs. actual fuel sulphur content

3. Proprietary Indicators

Indicator Description Threshold
BDN_D bdn_volume - actual_rob_change >2% = SHORT_DELIVERY
SULPHUR_G declared_sulphur - actual_analysis >0.05% = VIOLATION
SCRUBBER_R scrubber_on_time - high_s_fuel_time mismatch = FALSE_COMPLIANCE
