AGENT_LOG_MARFREIGHT — Maritime Freight Rate & Chartering Forensics

Market: Dry bulk $50B+, tanker $40B+, container $150B+ freight markets Key UK: Baltic Exchange (London), Clarksons, Simpson Spence Young

1. Mission

Detect freight rate manipulation, charter party non-compliance, BOL fraud, and shipping derivatives anomalies.

2. Detection Architecture

2.1 Freight Rate Manipulation

  • F1 — Phantom Surcharges: BAF, CAF, PSS charges with no cost basis
  • F2 — Spot vs Contract: Spot booking at contract rate or vice versa (profit skimming)
  • F3 — Index Manipulation: Self-reported rates to Baltic Exchange vs. actual transactions

2.2 Bill of Lading Fraud

  • B1 — Surrender BOL: Original BOL not surrendered at destination (cargo released without title)
  • B2 — Switch BOL: Multiple sets of BOL for same cargo (fraudulent sale to multiple buyers)
  • B3 — Electronic BOL: Bolero/essDOCS integrity verification

2.3 Charter Party Compliance

  • C1 — Laytime Calculation: Demurrage/despatch calculation vs. NOR, SOF, BIMCO clause
  • C2 — Speed/Consumption: Charter speed/consumption warranties vs. noon report data
  • C3 — Off-Hire: Off-hire events validity vs. vessel technical capability

3. Proprietary Indicators
