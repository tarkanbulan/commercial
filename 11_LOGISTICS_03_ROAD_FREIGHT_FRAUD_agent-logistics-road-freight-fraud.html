AGENT_LOG_FREIGHT — Road Freight Billing & Brokerage Forensics

Market: Freight brokerage $100B+. Invoice fraud estimated 5-10% of transactions.

1. Mission

Detect billing and brokerage fraud in road freight: double brokering, phantom loads, detention/delay claim manipulation.

2. Detection Architecture

2.1 Double Brokering Detection

  • D1 — Carrier Verification: Carrier dispatched vs. carrier who actually picked up (ELD/gate cross-ref)
  • D2 — Rate Disparity: Shipper-paid rate vs. carrier-received rate. >20% gap = double broker
  • D3 — Authority Check: Carrier MC/DOT/ O-licence authority verification

2.2 Phantom Load Detection

  • P1 — Load Tracking: Billed load with no corresponding GPS or pickup record
  • P2 — Duplicate Load: Same load billed twice to different customers
  • P3 — Weight/Volume Fraud: Billed weight > actual weight (scaled at receiver)

2.3 Detention/Delay Claim Verification

  • V1 — Wait Time: Claimed detention vs. facility gate-in/gate-out records
  • V2 — Layover: Overnight layover claims vs. driver hours log consistency
  • V3 — Lumper Service: Third-party loading/unloading claims vs. facility records

3. Proprietary Indicators

Indicator Description Threshold
BROKER_D carrier_dispatched - carrier_actual mismatch = DOUBLE_BROKER
PHANTOM_L billed_load_exists_in_gps FALSE = FRAUD
DETENTION_V claimed_hrs - facility_gate_hrs >30min = EXAGGERATED
