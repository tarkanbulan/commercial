UK Investigative Journalism & Civil Society RaaS Module

Source: T2SAIM Commercialization Framework v03 (§8 — Investigative Media and Research-as-a-Service) UK Context: Shrinking advertising revenues, contracting editorial staffs, rising individual publishers — yet UK media world-class (BBC, Guardian, Bureau of Investigative Journalism, The Times, Financial Times)

1. Mission

Research-as-a-Service subscription model for UK investigative journalism organizations. Process terabyte-scale UK public data (Companies House, Land Registry, Contracts Finder, parliamentary records) through T2SAIM's structural anomaly detection — giving small teams the analytical firepower of a major law firm.

2. Service Modules

2.1 Companies House Deep Investigation Service

  • C1 — PSC (Person with Significant Control) Network Mapping: Multi-company beneficial ownership graphs
  • C2 — Shell Company Lifecycle Analysis: Incorporation → shelf period → transaction → dissolution patterns
  • C3 — Director Overlap Detection: Same directors across hundreds of companies — "professional" directorship networks
  • C4 — Capital Structure Anomaly: £1 share capital companies with £M transaction volumes

2.2 UK Property / Land Ownership Forensics

  • L1 — Offshore Owner Mapping: UK property owned through offshore entities (Crown Dependencies, BVI, etc.)
  • L2 — Transaction Price Anomaly: Property sold below market value (money laundering indicator)
  • L3 — Political Exposure Scans: MPs, lords, councilors property portfolios vs. declared interests

2.3 Public Procurement Network Mapping

  • P1 — Single-Tender Award Concentration: Contracts awarded without competition → cronyism risk
  • P2 — Supplier-Politician Link Detection: Procurement winners linked to decision-makers
  • P3 — Value Deviation Analysis: Contract value vs. market benchmark → overpricing

2.4 UK Parliament/MPS Forensics

  • M1 — Register of Interests Integrity: Declared vs. undeclared interests via cross-reference
  • M2 — Parliamentary Voting Pattern Analysis: Division lobby correlation with financial interests
  • M3 — All-Party Parliamentary Group (APPG) Forensics: Secretarial/funding sources

2.5 Democracy / Civil Society Protection

  • D1 — SLAPP Litigation Detection: Strategic lawsuits against public participation — pattern recognition
  • D2 — Disinformation Source Tracing: Coordinated UK narrative attacks (Digital, Culture, Media & Sport committee concerns)
  • D3 — Civil Society Grant Integrity: Democracy fund / mega-foundation grant transparency
