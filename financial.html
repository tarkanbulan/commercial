UK Bribery Act Compliance, Offshore Asset Tracing & Forensic Accounting

Agent: Offshore Audit

Forensic analysis of trust structures, shell company networks, and cross-border fund movements. UK's Register of Overseas Entities (2022) revealed approximately £90B in previously undisclosed foreign property ownership in the UK.

Agent: Physical Asset Audit

Physical asset verification for anti-corruption: verifying government procurement, infrastructure project delivery, and asset registers. UK's National Audit Office estimates £4-6B annual fraud in public procurement.

UK Bribery Act Compliance

Section 7 creates strict liability for commercial organisations that fail to prevent bribery. Adequate procedures defence requires demonstrable forensic controls including cross-border due diligence, third-party risk assessment, and transaction monitoring.
