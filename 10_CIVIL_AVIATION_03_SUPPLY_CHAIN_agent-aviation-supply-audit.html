AGENT_AVIATION_SUPPLY — Aviation Supply Chain Integrity Module

1. Mission

Structural audit of aerospace supply chain performance. Detect delivery manipulation, supplier quality degradation, financial distress indicators, and logistics fraud in AOG (Aircraft on Ground) situations.

2. Detection Architecture

2.1 Supplier Performance Structural Audit

  • S1 — OTD Integrity: On-Time Delivery claims vs. actual production line need dates
  • S2 — Quality Escapement: Supplier quality defects vs. claimed defect rates. Escapement pattern analysis
  • S3 — Financial Health: Supplier financial statements vs. delivery reliability correlation

2.2 AOG Logistics Forensics

  • A1 — AOG Classification: Genuine AOG vs. routine demand classified as emergency
  • A2 — Emergency Pricing: AOG part pricing vs. standard pricing markup analysis
  • A3 — Rotable Pool Integrity: Rotable parts dispatched vs. returned ratio anomalies

2.3 Dual-Source Integrity

  • D1 — Source Verification: Parts sourced from approved vs. unapproved sources
  • D2 — Alternate Part Certification: PMA/STC parts vs. OEM part performance comparison

3. Proprietary Indicators

Indicator Description Threshold
OTD_R claimed_otd - actual_otd >5% = MISREPRESENTATION
AOG_F genuine_aog / total_aog_claims <0.8 = ABUSE
SUPPLIER_H financial_distress_zscore <1.8 = WARNING
