AGENT_LOG_COLDCHAIN — Cold Chain & Reefer Integrity Module

Market: Global cold chain logistics $300B+. Pharma cold chain $25B+. Regulatory: EU GDP (Good Distribution Practice), WHO, HACCP, ATP Agreement

1. Mission

Detect cold chain breaches, reefer temperature data manipulation, and delivery proof fraud in temperature-sensitive supply chains.

2. Detection Architecture

2.1 Temperature Breach Detection

  • T1 — Data Gap: Missing temp readings during transit. Points to intentional sensor disabling
  • T2 — Recovery Anomaly: Temperature out of range and rapid recovery (sensor manipulation indicator)
  • T3 — Seasonal Correlation: Breach rates vs. external temperature patterns

2.2 Reefer Data Integrity

  • R1 — Sensor Comparison: Multiple sensors in same unit showing different readings
  • R2 — Door Open Detection: Temperature spike correlating with door open events
  • R3 — Pre-Cooling: Unit not pre-cooled before loading (GDP violation)

2.3 Delivery Proof Forensics

  • D1 — POD Timestamp: Proof of delivery timestamp vs. GPS location at time
  • D2 — Signature Verification: Digital signature consistency across deliveries by same driver
  • D3 — Photo Metadata: Delivery photo timestamp/GPS vs. claimed delivery window

3. Proprietary Indicators

Indicator Description Threshold
BREACH_T time_outside_temp_range / total_transit >5% = CRITICAL
SENSOR_D sensor1_temp - sensor2_temp >2C = MANIPULATION
POD_G pod_gps - claimed_delivery_location >500m = FRAUD
