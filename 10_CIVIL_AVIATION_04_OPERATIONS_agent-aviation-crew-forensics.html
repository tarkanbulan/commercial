AGENT_AVIATION_CREW — Airline Crew Integrity Module

1. Mission

Detect crew scheduling manipulation, fatigue risk hiding, qualification fraud, and duty time compliance violations.

2. Detection Architecture

2.1 Fatigue Risk Detection

  • F1 — FTL Compliance: Actual duty hours vs. regulated limits (EASA FTL, CAP 371)
  • F2 — Circadian Disruption: Pattern of early starts, late finishes, and time zone crossings
  • F3 — Cumulative Fatigue: Consecutive duty days without adequate rest period

2.2 Qualification/Training Fraud

  • Q1 — License Validity: License check cycle vs. regulatory requirements
  • Q2 — Training Record: Simulator session timestamps vs. operator availability
  • Q3 — Medical Certificate: Medical expiration and restrictions tracking

2.3 Scheduling Manipulation

  • S1 — Pairing Construction: Pairings designed to circumvent rest requirements
  • S2 — Reserve Abuse: Reserve called at maximum legal duty to minimize pay
  • S3 — Bid Line Manipulation: Seniority-based bid system integrity

3. Proprietary Indicators

Indicator Description Threshold
FTL_R actual_duty / legal_max >0.95 = FATIGUE_RISK
CIRCADIAN early_starts + late_finishes in 7d >3 = HIGH_RISK
LICENSE_G license_valid_days_remaining <30 = EXPIRING
